# Product

## Register
Supermoda Online (Cali, Colombia)

## Users
Independent fashion entrepreneurs (resellers) and end consumers in Colombia looking to browse, download, and buy clothing, footwear, lingerie, beauty, and home items through high-quality catalogs.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a premium, highly conversion-oriented digital catalog catalog hub for Supermoda Online. It enables Colombian entrepreneurs to easily view, download, and order from 13+ virtual catalogs (Ryocco, Yanbal, Provocame, Unusual, Bata, St. Even, T&T, Ebba, Bartolomeo, Gianni Garetti, and Bata Kids) and consult sizing charts.

## Brand Personality
Professional, elegant, entrepreneur-focused, clean, and highly active.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, generic multi-category marketplaces.
- Static, boring text-only catalog pages.
- Broken link tables or outdated sizing images.

## Design Principles
- **Direct Conversion:** Lead with highly accessible "Download PDF" and "Order via WhatsApp" calls to action.
- **Supplier Authenticity:** Retain and highlight the official sizing guides provided by individual brands.
- **Progressive Discovery:** Offer quick search and filtering so users can instantly locate specific catalog brands.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- WCAG AA compliant.
- Support for keyboard navigation and screen readers.
- Clear contrast ratios for text overlays.
